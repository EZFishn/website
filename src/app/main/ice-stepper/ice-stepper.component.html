EZFishn Ice Stepper

The Ice Stepper is a portable Jigging Device that easily mounts to the bottom of a 5 gallon pail. The Ice Stepper runs off a 12 Volt battery, which is not included.

The Jigging Motion is created from a stepper motor and drive belt.

Simply, place your fishing line on the line holder and press the start switch and you will be jigging.
